About Advantage Chiropractic

Restoring well-being, without drugs or excuses.

After 23 years of practice in Longview, our doctors and team are dedicated to helping this community move better, hurt less, and rely a little less on medication for problems the body can often resolve on its own.

We focus on correcting the root cause of pain — through hands-on adjustment, movement, and honest guidance — rather than just easing the symptom in front of us. No overpromising, no pressure, just a clear plan you understand before you agree to it.

Our doctors are highly trained and specialized in injury recovery, pediatric care, prenatal care, athletic care, and whole-family wellness. We're deeply connected to this community, and that shapes how we practice.

A patient story

"By the time I got to my car, the migraine was gone."

After 37 years as a migraine sufferer — averaging 20+ migraines a month and trying nearly everything from Botox to a CPAP machine — Kathy Sharek tried chiropractic care as a last resort.

An x-ray showed her neck curvature at 17°, far below the normal 35°. After 12 weeks of tailored adjustments, twice a week, her curve improved by 10 degrees — and the frequency and severity of her migraines dropped dramatically.

"I no longer am afraid to go to a chiropractor. After an adjustment I feel a sense of release... I am so thankful for my chiropractor, Dr. Anik." — Kathy Sharek, Clatskanie, OR

What to expect on your first visit

We're here to determine if chiropractic care is right for you — and walk you through every step.

1

Exam & X-rays

A complete chiropractic examination including postural assessment and any necessary chiropractic x-rays.

2

Findings & adjustment

Your doctor reviews exam findings, explains the cause of your issue, and provides an adjustment specific to your needs.

3

Your care plan

We review your optimal care plan and package rates in detail — with a foundation of wellness care for the whole family.
